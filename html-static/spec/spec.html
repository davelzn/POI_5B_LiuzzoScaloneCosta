Kangaroo Island

Kangaroo Island, located in South Australia, is renowned for its unique wildlife, pristine beaches, and expansive national parks. A popular destination for spring and summer, the island offers activities such as wildlife tours, hiking, and relaxing on beautiful beaches. Ideal for a 2-3 day visit, Kangaroo Island provides a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation, with accommodations and experiences ranging from mid to high-end ($$-$$$). Its stunning natural beauty and well-preserved environment make it a must-visit spot, earning an impressive 4.7 rating.
